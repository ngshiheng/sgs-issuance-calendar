SGS Issuance Calendar

This calendar displays Google Calendar events for Singapore Government Securities (SGS) based on the MAS Auctions and Issuance Calendar. SGS includes SGS bonds, Singapore Savings Bonds (SSB), and Treasury bills (T-bills).

It retrieves SGS data such as SGS, SSB, and T-bills from MAS API and generates events of key dates related to SGS issuances.

To add this calendar to your own calendar, please click on the "+Google Calendar" icon.
